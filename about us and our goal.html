WHO ARE WE?

Hello, Greenologists!

Greenology is a Website built by a group of individuals who shares the same goal and mission to protect Earth by spreading awareness through its viewers. Greenology also aims to build a safe and healthy environment for everyone through the information they produce.

OUR GOAL
  • This website raises public awareness about the state of our ecosystem and environment.
  • For raising public awareness about the need for environmental protection.
  • For us ensure that all applicable environmental regulations are met.
  • For students to learn about various environmental protection strategies and methods.
  • For students to have a better understanding of our surroundings
  • Students will be able to understand how our environment or planet operates as a result of our environmental facts
  • For students to make environmental efforts that focus on ecosystem recovery, biodiversity conservation, the natural and cultural environment, human health, efficient material cycles free of hazardous chemicals, sustainable natural resource use, efficient energy usage, and consumption habits.
  • For students to find learning about the environment enjoyable and interesting.
